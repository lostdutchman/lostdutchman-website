Game Play

Nice Bowling is an unfair bowling game that was inspired by Wii Sports and Wario Ware.

Randomly selected effects are applied to the ball, pins, lane, or environment at the start of each frame. That is each Bowling frame, even I think changing everything about the game every time the GPU re-draws the screen would be too much.

The game featured pass and play multiplayer, the worst kind of multiplayer.

There is a high score system. I thought it would add to the absurdity since your score will mainly be luck.

The soft body bowling ball is my favorite effect.

About

This was the first videogame I made. I ended up releasing it on Google Play and Amazon. It can still be purchased on Amazon but Google wanted me to put the privacy policy into the game itself and the game was already on an outdated version of Unity 3D that Google Play no longer supported. I originaly planned to release a PC version but the controls on the game are bad, and I was sick of working on them so I decided to work on other projects. After I abandond Nice Bowling my friends 11 year old son was restless in a resturant and his dad suggested playing 'the bowling game' which made the boy cry. LOL get good kid! Since I am not working on this project and have not worked on it in years I am opening the games source code and inviting any fools on the internet to make a better version. We need more games that make children cry, if you do something with my game let me know and I'll play it. I'll also link to it here.
