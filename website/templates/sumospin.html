Nostalgic Graphics

Inspired by Atari and early Nintendo games. Bask in its sweet & chunky pixel goodness and CRT effects. If you collide with your apponent with enough force it simulates hitting a CRT and can change the static level.

One-Button Gameplay

When the button is not pressed you spin. Press the button to stop spinning and charge your attack. Release to launch forward. Hold the button too long and you over charge, this leaves you vulnerable for a short time but allows you to cancel an attack.

King of the Hill

Knock the opponent out of the ring to win the match. Win 3 matches to win the game.
